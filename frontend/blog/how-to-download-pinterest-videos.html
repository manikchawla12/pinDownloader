How to Download Pinterest Videos (2026 Comprehensive Guide)

By PinClip Editorial • Published March 2026 • 5 min read

Pinterest is arguably the most profoundly visual social media network on the internet today. What started as a platform heavily dominated by static images and infographics has dramatically evolved. In 2026, Pinterest is bursting with dynamic, high-quality video content ranging from 60-second cooking tutorials to expansive DIY home renovation guides. But what happens when you discover a video so useful that you want to save it permanently, offline?

Unfortunately, the Pinterest app does not provide a native "Download to Gallery" button for videos. While you can save pins to your in-app boards, accessing them still requires an internet connection and the risk that the original creator might delete the video. That's why millions of users turn to third-party tools to download Pinterest videos.

Why Download Pinterest Videos Locally?

There are numerous, perfectly valid reasons for wanting local MP4 access to Pinterest visual content:

  • Offline Inspiration: If you are traveling on a flight without Wi-Fi, having that perfect gardening tutorial saved can make an hour pass by rapidly.
  • Content Archival: Digital rot is real. Creators frequently purge their old content. If you base your wedding planning off a specific reel, you want physical ownership of that reference.
  • Sharing with Non-Users: Believe it or not, not everyone has a Pinterest account. Sending a direct MP4 file via WhatsApp or iMessage bypasses the annoyance of forcing someone to log in just to view a single clip.
