Pinterest is one of the best platforms for discovering creative ideas, tutorials, recipes, and inspiration. Millions of users browse Pinterest daily and come across videos they want to keep — whether it's a step-by-step cooking tutorial, a DIY home project, or a workout routine they want to follow at the gym.

The problem? Pinterest doesn't let you download videos natively. You can save pins to your boards, but that requires an internet connection and the Pinterest app. If the creator deletes the video, your saved pin disappears too.

Why Download Pinterest Videos?

Downloading Pinterest videos can be useful in many everyday situations:

  • Save tutorials for offline use: Follow cooking recipes in the kitchen or craft tutorials in the garage without needing Wi-Fi.
  • Watch videos without internet: Perfect for flights, commutes, or travel to areas with limited connectivity.
  • Share content with friends: Send videos directly via WhatsApp, iMessage, or Telegram to people who don't use Pinterest.
  • Keep inspiration for future projects: Build an organized offline library of wedding ideas, home decor, fashion, and more.
  • Educational reference: Students and teachers can save instructional content for classroom use or study sessions.

Whether you're a student, content creator, or casual user, having videos saved locally on your device is incredibly convenient.

Method 2: Download Pinterest Videos on iPhone

Downloading videos on iPhone requires a few extra steps due to iOS restrictions on file handling. Here's the specific process for iPhone and iPad users:

Steps:

  1. Copy the Pinterest video link from the Pinterest app (tap Share → Copy Link).
  2. Open Safari browser on your iPhone.
  3. Visit PinClip — type the URL in Safari's address bar.
  4. Paste the link into the download box and tap Download.
  5. Save the video — when the download button appears, tap it. The file will be saved to your Files app under Downloads.

💡 Pro Tip: Use Safari instead of Chrome for better download compatibility on iOS. If the video opens in a new tab instead of downloading, tap and hold on the video, then select "Save to Files" or "Save Video" from the context menu.

How to Move the Video to Camera Roll

After saving to your Files app:

  1. Open the Files app on your iPhone.
  2. Navigate to Downloads folder.
  3. Tap and hold the video file.
  4. Select Share → Save Video.

The video will now appear in your Photos/Camera Roll app, ready to view or share.

Common Issues & Fixes

Video not downloading?

  • Make sure the link is a valid Pinterest URL (should start with https://www.pinterest.com/pin/ or https://pin.it/).
  • Ensure the pin is public — private or restricted pins cannot be downloaded.
  • Verify the pin actually contains a video, not just a static image.

Download button not working?

  • Refresh the page and try again.
  • Try using a different browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox).
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • If on iPhone, make sure you're using Safari for best compatibility.

Video quality is low?

  • PinClip always downloads the highest resolution available. The quality depends on what the original creator uploaded.
  • Most Pinterest videos are available in 720p or 1080p HD.
