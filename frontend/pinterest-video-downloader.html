The Ultimate Guide to Saving Pinterest Videos

When browsing Pinterest, you've likely encountered brilliant short clips — a perfectly styled room tour, a satisfying craft tutorial, or a mouthwatering recipe demonstration — that you wanted to keep permanently. Pinterest's native sharing options let you save content to your boards, but that only works within the app and requires an internet connection. What if you want the actual video file on your device?

That's where PinClip comes in. Our Pinterest video downloader was built exclusively for this purpose: to extract the original, high-quality video file from any Pinterest post and deliver it to you as a standard MP4 file that works on any device, any media player, and any operating system.

What Can PinClip Download?

PinClip supports the full range of Pinterest video content:

  • Standard video pins: Regular video posts uploaded by users and brands. These are typically landscape or square format.
  • Idea Pins: Pinterest's multi-page story format, which can contain video clips alongside images and text. PinClip extracts the video portion.
  • Pinterest Reels: Short-form vertical videos similar to Instagram Reels or TikTok. Visit our dedicated Reels downloader for more info.
  • Promoted Pins: Even sponsored video content from brands can be saved using PinClip, as long as the content is publicly accessible.
