About PinClip

Our Mission

PinClip is a free online tool designed to help users download Pinterest videos in high quality. Our mission is simple: to provide the most reliable, fastest, and safest way to save Pinterest video content directly to any device — without requiring software installations, account signups, or compromising on video quality.

We believe that when you find inspiration on Pinterest — whether it's a cooking recipe that you want to try offline in the kitchen, a DIY tutorial for a weekend project, or a workout routine you want to follow at the gym — you should be able to save it easily. Pinterest's native sharing options don't include direct video downloads, and we built PinClip to bridge that gap.

Why We Built PinClip

Our team of developers and designers noticed a persistent problem: existing video download tools were either riddled with intrusive pop-up ads, required installing browser extensions of questionable safety, or simply didn't work reliably with Pinterest's unique video infrastructure. Many tools would return low-quality versions or fail silently on Idea Pins and Reels.

We set out to build something better. PinClip was engineered from the ground up with a deep understanding of Pinterest's content delivery architecture, including how the platform serves HLS adaptive streams and signed media URLs. The result is a tool with a significantly higher success rate and better output quality than generic alternatives.

Our Core Values

  • Privacy First: We do not store any downloaded videos on our servers. We don't track your download history, and we never ask for personal information. Your privacy matters to us.
  • Quality Without Compromise: We always extract the highest available resolution. If a video was uploaded in 1080p, that's exactly what you'll receive — no downscaling, no re-encoding.
  • Accessibility: PinClip works on every major platform and device. Whether you're using an iPhone, an older Android device, a Chromebook, or a desktop Linux machine, PinClip is accessible through any modern web browser.
  • Transparency: We're upfront about what our tool does and how it works. You can read our Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Disclaimer for complete details.

Responsible Use

PinClip is intended exclusively for personal, non-commercial use. We encourage all users to respect the intellectual property rights of content creators on Pinterest. If you plan to use downloaded content in any public or commercial context, please obtain proper permission from the original creator first. For more details, please review our Disclaimer page.
