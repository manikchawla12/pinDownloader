About Us

We built PinClip to solve a simple problem: there was no easy way to save inspirational Pinterest videos locally without watermarks or quality loss. Our team of passionate developers created this free, fast, and secure tool for personal use and archiving purposes. We believe in providing an amazing user experience with a clean design, zero intrusive pop-ups, and the fastest processing speeds possible.
