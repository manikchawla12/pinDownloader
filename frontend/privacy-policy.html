Privacy Policy

At PinClip, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy details the information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect or store any personal information from users who visit our site. You can use our tool without registering an account. The URLs you paste into our downloader are processed temporarily and are not stored in our database.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) and advertising networks (such as Google AdSense). These third-party services use cookies to improve your experience and display relevant ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.

3. Logs and Security

We may log non-identifiable data such as IP addresses, browser types, and access times for error troubleshooting and security purposes. This data is kept strictly confidential and periodically deleted.

4. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
